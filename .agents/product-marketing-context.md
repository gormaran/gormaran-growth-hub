*Last updated: 2026-04-07*

## Product Overview
**One-liner:** Gormaran is an AI-powered growth platform with 30+ pre-built tools that turns hours of marketing work into minutes — without prompt engineering.
**What it does:** Gormaran gives marketers, founders, and agencies structured AI tools that produce professional, publish-ready outputs across content, SEO, ads, strategy, and more. Unlike generic AI tools (ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude), every tool has a purpose-built workflow that eliminates trial-and-error prompting and endless editing.

## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** Generic AI tools require constant prompt engineering, produce inconsistent outputs, and take almost as long to clean up as doing the work manually.
**Why alternatives fall short:**
- **ChatGPT/Gemini/Claude (direct)**: Require expertise to prompt well; outputs are generic and need heavy editing; no workflow structure
- **Traditional copywriters/agencies**: Expensive, slow turnaround, not scalable
- **Other AI tools**: Either too narrow (one use case) or too generic (no structure)
**What it costs them:** 3–10 hours per week on content creation; thousands of euros per year on freelancers or agencies
**Emotional tension:** Overwhelm from the volume of marketing tasks; fear of falling behind competitors who are moving faster

## Competitive Landscape
**Direct:** Jasper, Copy.ai, Writesonic — falls short because they're writing tools only, no strategy/planning tools, and still require good prompts
**Secondary:** Hiring freelancers or agencies — falls short because expensive, slow, and doesn't scale
**Indirect:** DIY with ChatGPT/Claude — falls short because no structure, inconsistent quality, high learning curve

## Differentiation
**Key differentiators:**
- 30+ purpose-built tools covering 10 business categories (not just copywriting)
- Structured workflows produce publish-ready outputs — no editing required
- Covers the full growth stack: content + SEO + ads + strategy + finance + automation
